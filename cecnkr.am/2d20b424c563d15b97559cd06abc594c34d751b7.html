The deputies of the newly elected National Assembly have been registered and received certificates

18.05.2015

     On May 18, 2015 the regular meeting of the NKR Central Electoral Commission was held under the chairmanship of the Commission Chairperson Srbuhi Arzumanyan.

     The meeting was attended by NKR Central Electoral Commission with its full complement, members of the newly elected NKR National Assembly, chairmen of territorial electoral commissions of the NKR, representatives of the media were invited.

     Before proceeding to the agenda item, Srbuhi Arzumanyan on behalf of the Central Electoral Commission of NKR congratulated deputies in connection with the election, and on the Day of Liberation of Berdzor, then the attendees  honored the memory of the fallen defenders of the Fatherland  with a minute of silence.

     The only issue on the agenda of the meeting concerned the registration of  the deputies.

     In accordance with the 12th paragraph of part 1 of Article 48,  the 6th and 7th Parts of the 123rd article of the NKR Electoral Code and N 83-I decision of May 10, 2015 of the Central Electoral Commission of NKR ” On the elections  of the deputies  by the results of the Nagorno Karabakh Republic National Assembly elections of May 3, 2015 on the  proportional electoral system and taking into account the applicatins of Arayik Harutyunyan (the party of “Free Motherland”), Suren Grigorian (“Democratic Party of Artsakh”), Arthur Aghabekyan (Party “Armenian Revolutionary Federation”) and Valery Balayan (Party “Armenian Revolutionary Federation Dashnaktsutiun “) to abandon the deputy mandate included in the electoral lists of parties that won at least 5 percent cast” yes “ballots from the amount of the inaccuracies and the total number of ballot “Yes” ballots and took part in  May 3, 2015 regular elections to the National Assembly of the Nagorno Karabakh Republic by the proportional electoral system, and the fact of the election on majoritarian system of Arthur Tovmasyan , Arzik Mkhitaryan (the party of” Free Motherland ” ), Vahram Balayan (Party “Armenian Revolutionary Federation”) and Vitaly Balasanyan (the party “Movement-88″), included in the electoral lists of parties, NKR Central Electoral Commission has made a protocol at the meeting on registration of the NKR National Assembly deputies by proportional system and providing them certificates of a deputy.

    At the end of the meeting, led by the provisions of Part 1 of Article 34 of the NKR law “National Assembly Regulation” the NKR Central Electoral Commission announced that on May 21, 2015. at 11.00a.m. the first session of the newly elected NKR National Assembly of the 6th convocation convened.
